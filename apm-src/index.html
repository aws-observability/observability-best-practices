Welcome

This documentation site covers CloudWatch Application Performance Monitoring (APM) for developers, operators, and architects who need to understand application behavior in production.

APM helps you reduce time-to-detect and time-to-resolve by correlating service health (latency, errors, availability, and throughput) with traces and related signals across your architecture.
