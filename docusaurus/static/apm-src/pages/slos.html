Service Level Objectives (SLOs)

Why SLOs matter

SLOs let you express reliability and performance targets (for example, “99.9% availability” or “p99 latency under 500ms”) and track them over time. In Application Signals, SLO/SLI health is surfaced in the Services page and Application Map to speed up triage.

What you can create SLOs on

CloudWatch SLOs are built on metrics. Application Signals’ standard metrics (latency, fault, error, availability) are common SLI inputs.

Official docs: Service level objectives (SLOs).

Typical workflow

  • Pick a service and decide which indicator best reflects user impact (availability vs latency).
  • Define the objective (target, time window, and evaluation).
  • Alert using alarms on SLO/SLI status or burn rate.
