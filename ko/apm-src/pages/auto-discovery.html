CloudWatch APM Documentation

Auto Discovery

What Application Signals discovers

After you enable Application Signals and your services emit telemetry, CloudWatch automatically discovers and displays:

  • Services and their operations
  • Dependencies (downstream calls)
  • Topology (Application Map)

Discovery is driven by metrics and traces emitted within the selected time range (for example, the default “past three hours”). If a service has no activity in the time range, it won’t be shown.

One-time account setup (service discovery)

The first time you start service discovery in an account, CloudWatch creates the service-linked role AWSServiceRoleForCloudWatchApplicationSignals. This role allows CloudWatch to discover and correlate services.

Official steps: Enable Application Signals in your account.

Operational expectations

  • Discovery latency: topology and service discovery can be delayed by up to ~10 minutes.
  • SLO/SLI evaluation: SLI health evaluation can be delayed (for example, up to ~15 minutes).
  • Scale limits: up to 1,000 services can be displayed in the console.
